Biography

RHYMA (Charlie Inyang)

RHYMA, a Nigerian-born recording artist based in Gauteng, South Africa, is redefining African urban music. Known for his dynamic blend of Afrobeats, Pop, Commercial House, and New Age sounds, he represents a new generation of artists pushing boundaries while honoring their African heritage.

From his beginnings with the a cappella group Soul Healers to launching his solo career in 2014, RHYMA has crafted a versatile sound inspired by legends like Fela Kuti, Brenda Fassie, and Bob Marley. His music resonates with themes of love, ambition, and freedom.

His discography includes the debut album 'Fly With You' (2019) and EPs 'Ambition' (2020) and 'Vibes on Vibes' (2025). He kicked off 2026 with the single 'Medusa', featuring Sjijo and Jack Jakalas, exploring the eccentric 3-Step sound.

"Music is not just sound, it's an architecture of emotion."
